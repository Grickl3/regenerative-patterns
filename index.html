Patterns for Regenerative Changemaking

Broaden your social design palette with this pattern language originally published by Adam Brock in his 2017 release, Change Here Now. Grab a copy for a more robust explanation and a read that will leave you truly believing that we can solve our own problems.

Each pattern is assigned to a deck which corresponds to its domain in social design. Just select the pattern types which pertain to your project and those decks will automatically shuffle themselves together. Then mash that Draw Card button. You'll get a randomly selected pattern to contemplate in the context of your project.
